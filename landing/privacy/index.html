Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Divera ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Divera mobile application for iOS (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and any profile information you choose to provide.

Photos

When you use our AI species identification feature, you may upload underwater photos. These photos are processed to identify marine species. Photos you choose to share are visible to other users in the community feed.

Dive Logs

When you log a dive, we store details you provide such as depth, conditions, date, location, photos, and species encountered.

Location Data

With your permission, we access your device location to show nearby dive sites and dive centers on the map. Location data is only accessed when you use map features and is not continuously tracked in the background.

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the App, such as which features are used and general app performance metrics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the App's features, including AI species identification
  • To display your dive logs, species sightings, and shared content
  • To show you relevant dive sites and dive centers near your location
  • To enable community features such as the activity feed
  • To send you important service communications (e.g., account verification)
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the App

3. AI Species Identification

When you use our AI fish identification feature, your photos are processed using machine learning models to identify marine species. Photos submitted for identification are used solely for providing you with identification results. We do not use your personal photos to train AI models without your explicit consent.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data in the following cases:

  • Community features: Dive logs, species sightings, and photos you choose to share publicly are visible to other Divera users.
  • Service providers: We use third-party services to host and operate the App (e.g., cloud infrastructure, analytics). These providers only access data as needed to perform their services and are bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing (e.g., location access)
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., dive centers). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.
